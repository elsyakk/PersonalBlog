    • the road,
    ahead

    One time when I was browsing through countless books on goodreads, I
    came across a book by Bill Gates written, “The road ahead” with a long road
    as the cover of the book. It was electric for me, there is just something about
    that title and book cover that brings me into the core of my endeavors. The
    road, ahead of

    What came across from that book is that the end of the road is not visible
    from where the photo was taken. The road looks never ends. It struck into
    me, I feel like a lot of the human ambitions are just a long road, achieving
    one or another, or even encountering setbacks on the road. We cannot
    predict what’s out there. We can only focus on the present, whether we are
    capable enough to pass the road, no matter how bumpy, or how long it takes
    to the perceived end goal

    The name end goal is not necessarily true, there is no end goal to the human
    imagination and dream, we are truly a greedy souls.

    To me, the meaning of “The Road Ahead” is the limitless of the human
    accomplishment, even each of us. It’s a way to see life differently, beyond the
    pointless 9-to-5 job that most people ended up with. It is a representation of
    a long journey and the limitless dream that we can have
